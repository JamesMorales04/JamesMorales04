## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a bilingual Senior .NET Backend Engineer with over 5 years of experience building scalable enterprise solutions across finance, automotive, and e-commerce. I specialize in **C#**, **ASP.NET Core**, and **Azure**, applying clean architecture and domain-driven design to ship resilient, maintainable systems.

Currently, I work as a backend-oriented full-stack developer at **Perficient**, delivering for Fortune 500 clients. I also enjoy improving product quality through testing, automation, and thoughtful system design.

I have hands-on experience applying large language models (LLMs) in production systems and building internal tools and dashboards that support engineering teams.

- 🎓 Software Engineering degree from **EAFIT University**

- 📬 Open to collaboration and interesting opportunities
